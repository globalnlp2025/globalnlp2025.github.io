GlobalNLP2025 @ RANLP 2025

Beyond English: Natural Language Processing for All Languages in an Era of Large Language Models
(GlobalNLP 2025: Breaking Boundaries With LLMs)

Venue and Date

The first Workshop on "Beyond English: Natural Language Processing for All Languages in an Era of Large Language Models" (GlobalNLP 2025) will be held at the RANLP 2025 conference in Varna, Bulgaria, on 11-13 September 2025.

Objectives

Natural Language Processing (NLP) has advanced dramatically with the introduction of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Generative AI, but many languages remain underserved. This workshop aims to explore creative, data-efficient, and multilingual strategies to promote inclusive NLP advancement.

  • Data-Efficient NLP (transfer learning, few-shot and zero-shot learning)
  • Multilingual and Cross-Lingual Models
  • Semantic-Based Approaches
  • Linked Data and Knowledge Graphs
  • Real-World Applications (education, healthcare, government, etc.)
  • Corpus Creation Tools and Linguistic Resources
  • Reusability of Linguistic Resources

Target Audience

GlobalNLP2025 targets NLP researchers, linguists, industry practitioners, and AI developers, particularly those working in resource-constrained environments. Contributors may come from academia, research institutes, tech companies, and non-profits specializing in language technology.

Workshop Format

  • Keynote talks by prominent researchers
  • Paper presentations (oral and poster sessions)
  • Panel discussion on multilingual LLM challenges
  • Interactive demos and practical applications
  • All accepted papers published in ACL proceedings
